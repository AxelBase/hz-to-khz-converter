Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 06, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Hz to kHz Converter, a simple, client-side web utility. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and operate on a strict privacy-first principle.

Because the converter runs entirely in your browser, we do not collect, store, or process any personal data. All calculations happen locally on your device. This policy is designed to be transparent about our zero-data-collection approach.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The Hz to kHz Converter does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII) — names, email addresses, or any contact details.
  • Usage Data — IP addresses, browser type, device information, or location data.
  • Input Data — any frequency values you enter in Hz or kHz fields.
  • Analytics or Tracking Data — we do not use cookies, Google Analytics, pixels, or any tracking technologies.

All conversion operations are performed client-side only. Your entered values never leave your device.

3. How the Tool Works

When you use the converter:

  • You type values directly into the input fields.
  • Real-time bidirectional conversion happens instantly using JavaScript in your browser.
  • Results are displayed to five decimal places without any data being sent to external servers.
  • The tool is completely stateless — no data is saved between sessions.

Your frequency inputs remain private at all times.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party services that collect data. The site uses Bootstrap via CDN for styling only. No analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts are present. Any interaction with the page stays entirely on your device.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The Hz to kHz Converter does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. Your session is fully stateless and private.

6. Data Security

Although we collect no data, we recommend you follow good security practices:

  • Use up-to-date browsers and operating systems.
  • Clear browser data when working with sensitive calculations.
  • Avoid using the tool on shared or public computers for confidential work.

7. Children’s Privacy

This tool is suitable for general audiences and not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. International Users

Since no personal data is collected, the tool complies by design with major privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws worldwide.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the tool after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out via the GitHub repository linked in the footer.

This Privacy Policy reflects our strong commitment to zero data collection and user privacy. By using the Hz to kHz Converter, you agree to these practices.
